The Problem

Gift-giving is a meaningful gesture that symbolizes relationships and appreciation. However, finding the perfect gift can be stressful and complex, especially during busy seasons like Christmas.

Our Solution

Giftly is a web app that leverages AI to help users quickly identify and purchase ideal gifts for friends and family for every occasion.

How It Works

  • Describe your gift criteria through conversation with our AI
  • Use selection boxes for context (celebration, age, relationship)
  • Upload an image of a gift you have in mind
  • Select from existing user profiles with stored preferences

Key Features

  • AI-powered gift recommendations using OpenAI's LLM
  • Multi-vendor product search across Amazon, AliExpress, Shopify, and eBay
  • Interactive chat to refine gift suggestions
  • Secret Santa game organization (3-10 participants)
  • User profiles and wishlists

Our Advantage

Giftly combines AI-powered recommendations with a comprehensive search across multiple online vendors, offering a unique and efficient gift-finding experience.

Why Choose Giftly?

  • Save time and reduce stress in gift selection
  • Find personalized gifts for any occasion
  • Explore options from multiple vendors in one place
  • Enjoy the gift-giving process without the hassle
